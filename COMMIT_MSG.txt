Phase 16: Bidirectional volume/geometry shadows + chained VolMerge fix

Bidirectional Shadows (CPU):
- Surface shadow rays march through volumes (cloud shadow on ground)
  Beer-Lambert extinction, 32 coarse steps per volume, both primary + bounce
- Volume shadow rays trace BVH geometry (building shadow in fog)
  Opaque geometry fully blocks, glass partial block by opacity
- Volume parameters passed to _ShadeSpectral for shadow access

Bidirectional Shadows (GPU):
- Surface shadow rays march through gpuVolumes[] along light direction
  Forward declarations for sampleDensity/worldToVolUV
- Volume shadow rays call optixTrace from marchSingleVolume
  One trace per light per step, guarded by hasRealGeometry flag
- hasRealGeometry flag distinguishes real geometry from dummy GAS
  Volume-only scenes skip all geometry shadow optixTrace calls
- Applied to both scene lights and HDRI virtual lights

Chained VolMerge fix:
- GetVolumes recursively collects from child SpectralVolMerge inputs
- VolMergeEntry now carries vdbRead pointer for material lookup
- Material walk uses per-entry vdbRead->input(0) to find SpectralVolumeMaterial
  Works across any depth of VolMerge chaining
- Fallback to positional matching from top-level VolMerge if no mat input
- Child VolMerge lights also inherited

       SpectralGPU.h/.cpp, SpectralVolMerge.h/.cpp, SpectralRenderIop.cpp
